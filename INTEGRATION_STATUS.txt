========================================
FIREBASE COLLECTIONS STRUCTURE:
========================================

Your Firestore will have:

1. production_orders
   - orderId, customer, product, quantity
   - status, dueDate, userId, createdAt

2. analytics
   - userId, date, revenue, production
   - efficiency, costs, type

3. machines
   - userId, machineName, efficiency
   - capacity, used, status, lastUpdated

4. innovation_projects
   - userId, name, status, category
   - progress, startDate, createdAt

5. equipment
   - userId, name, age, condition
   - efficiency, status, createdAt

6. infrastructure_scores
   - userId, overallScore, equipmentHealth
   - energyEfficiency, date
